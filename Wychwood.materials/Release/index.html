Wychwood

You have just been released from prison where
  you shared a cell with Jonesy.
   Unaware that he was talking in his sleep, Jonesy
     gives you a very good idea of where he hid
      the last from a bank robbery
       You are sure it is hidden somewhere near
         Wychwood Manor. To a man with your
          criminal talents this should be a doddle.
           But......nothing is ever as simple as it seems

Wychwood

Wychwood was originally released in 1985 by Geoff Larsen of the British company Larsoft.

It was originally created with the use of The Quill. This implementation is based on is Version E4.

An emulated version of the original BBC Micro game is available online in the Complete BBC Micro Games Archive.

A walkthrough is available at The Classic Adventures Solution Archive.

This version was implemented by ferkung in 2022 in Inform 7 and has IFID 737E14C7-3C0E-4FA4-9280-A2453694F808.
